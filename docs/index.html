About

BRIDGE (Brain Reconstruction Integrating Distibuted Graph Estimates) is a pipeline for reconstructing white matter fiber pathways from high-resolution 3D synchrotron microscopy images.

Developed as part of the Human Organ Atlas and CONNECTS Centre for Large-scale Imaging of Neural Circuits (LINC), BRIDGE combines GPU-accelerated processing with scalable graph algorithms to handle terabyte-scale volumes while preserving biological topology.
