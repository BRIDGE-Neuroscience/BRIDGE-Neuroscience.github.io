About

BRIDGE (Brain Reconstruction via Iterative Directed Graph Extraction) is a pipeline for reconstructing white matter fiber pathways from high-resolution 3D synchrotron microscopy images.

Developed as part of the NIH CONNECTS Centre, BRIDGE combines GPU-accelerated processing with parameter-free graph algorithms to handle terabyte-scale volumes while preserving biological topology.
